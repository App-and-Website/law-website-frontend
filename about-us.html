Experienced Legal Advisors Serving Lebanon & the Middle East

Assaad Ataya Law Firm is a distinguished legal practice providing comprehensive legal and consultancy services throughout Lebanon and the wider Middle Eastern region. Under the leadership of Maitre Assaad Ataya, the firm delivers strategic, practical, and results-focused legal solutions for individuals, businesses, and organizations.

We are committed to offering clients comprehensive support across litigation, corporate and commercial matters, real estate, family and more. We handle every case with precision, confidentiality, and a strong focus on protecting our clients’ interests. Additionally, we provide specialized Australian immigration assistance in collaboration with Rifat.

Our Mission

At Assaad Ataya Law Firm, our mission is to provide strategic, reliable, and results-driven legal solutions that protect the rights and interests of individuals, businesses, and organizations. We are committed to delivering clear guidance, practical advice, and strong legal representation. Through dedicated client service, ethical practice, and precise legal analysis, we aim to simplify complex legal challenges and offer trusted support at every step.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading law firm recognized for excellence, integrity, and innovation in legal services across Lebanon and the wider Arab region. We strive to set new standards in professional advocacy, client support, and cross-border legal solutions. By continuously enhancing our expertise and expanding our regional capabilities, we aim to become the preferred legal partner for clients and seamless assistance.
